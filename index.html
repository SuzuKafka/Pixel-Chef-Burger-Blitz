    🍔 Pixel Chef: Burger Blitz

    How to Play

    1. Move the tray with ◀︎▶︎ (or A/D).
    2. Catch ingredients in the order shown in the Order card.
    3. Avoid Rotten items — they cost -1 ❤️.
    4. Golden Patty grants +1 ❤️.
    5. Press Enter to start, P to pause.
    6. Endless mode: play until lives run out.
